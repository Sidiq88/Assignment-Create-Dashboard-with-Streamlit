# Memahami Dataset
Identifikasi variabel utama seperti Customer ID, transaksi, frekuensi pembelian, total belanja.
Lakukan pembersihan data (data cleaning).

Column Name Description 
Row_Id A unique identifier for each row in the dataset. 
Order_Id A unique identifier for each order placed in the store. 
Order_Date The date when the order was placed. 
Ship_Date The date when the order was shipped to the customer. 
Ship_Mode The method of shipment used for delivering the order (e.g., "Second Class"). 
Customer_Id A unique identifier for each customer in the dataset. 
Customer_Name The name of the customer who placed the order. 
Segment The market segment to which the customer belongs (e.g., "Consumer"). 
Country The country where the order was placed. 
City The city where the order was placed. 
State The state where the order was placed. 
Postal_Code The postal or ZIP code of the customer's location. 
Region The region within the country where the order was placed (e.g., "South"). 
Product_Id A unique identifier for each product sold. 
Category The high-level category of the product (e.g., "Furniture"). 
Subcategory A more specific subcategory of the product (e.g., "Bookcases"). 
Product_Name 
Sales 
The name of the product sold. 
The total revenue generated from the sale of the product (in monetary units). 
Quantity 
The number of units of the product sold in the order. 
Discount 
The discount applied to the sale (as a proportion, e.g., 0.1 for 10%). 
Profit 
The profit earned from the sale after accounting for costs and discounts. 

# Performa Penjualan Superstore
1. Office Supplies adalah kebutuhan rutin dengan frekuensi pembelian tinggi, 
sementara Technology bernilai besar tapi lebih jarang dibeli. 
2. Pelanggan lebih mengutamakan biaya pengiriman rendah (Standard Class) 
dibanding kecepatan. 
3. Musiman (seasonality) memengaruhi perilaku belanja. Puncak bisa terkait 
musim belanja akhir tahun atau promo tertentu.
